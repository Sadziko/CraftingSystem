CONTROLS:
Movement: WASD/Arrows
Collect item from source: G
Open equipment: E

-Dropped items pick themself up automaticly
-Hovering item in inventory panel shows 2 buttons: Drop and Add to craft
-When Item is added to craft panel, you can hover over it to show Return to inventory button (same icon as drop)
-When 2 Items are added to craft panel you can press button in the middle to give crafting a try, depending on the result correct particle system will play
